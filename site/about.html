About TOP Compass

TOP Compass explains Singapore's building-completion process — the Temporary Occupation Permit and everything around it — for the people it affects: home buyers on one side, and the professionals who deliver buildings on the other.

How this site is funded

TOP Compass may earn revenue from: bank referral programmes when readers use links to mortgage products; flat referral fees from independent defect-inspection firms; flat-fee sponsorships and directory listings from industry firms, which appear only in the professional section of this site; and sales of paid professional resources.

Editorial rules

  • No sponsor influences, previews or pays for the placement or content of any guide or tool on this site.
  • Buyer-facing pages never carry industry sponsorship. The liquidated-damages calculator and buyer guides state what the law and standard contracts prescribe — nothing more, and no less because of who advertises here.
  • Referral links are disclosed, and referrals are never success-fee arrangements with property agents or licensed professionals.
  • Personal data submitted on this site is used only for the purpose stated at the point of collection and is never shared across audiences or with third parties without express consent (PDPA).

Accuracy

Content states its verification date. Singapore's building-control regime changes often — the CORENET X transition runs through 2027 — and this site is refreshed against BCA, URA, SCDF and PUB circulars quarterly. Found an error? Please report it; corrections are published.

What this site is not

Nothing here is legal, financial, or professional engineering/architectural advice, and nothing here creates a professional engagement. For decisions about your property or project, engage your solicitor, banker, or a registered Qualified Person.
